AI Search FAQs

Why does my website need AI Search Visibility?

+
Your website needs AI Search Visibility because over 40% of search traffic is transitioning to AI answer engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and Gemini. Without structured schemas and direct Q&A formatting, AI crawlers will overlook your content and cite competitors instead.

What is an llms.txt file and do you configure it?

+
Yes, we configure and install llms.txt files at your root directory to serve as a machine-readable markdown map for LLM crawlers. This file guides AI agents directly to clear, structured summaries of your website content, speeding up accurate ingestion.

How long does it take to see results in AI answers?

+
Websites often see improved citation ranking and query relevance inside AI answers within 3 to 6 weeks. While traditional SEO takes months, AI models frequently refresh their context caches and index updated entity schemas quickly.
