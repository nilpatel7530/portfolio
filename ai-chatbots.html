What is an AI Chatbot?

Unlike outdated chatbots that operate strictly on static IF/THEN rules, modern AI Chatbots utilize advanced semantic models. They parse user intentions, handle grammar variations, adapt tone to context, query knowledge bases in real-time, and transfer conversations to live humans when needed.

AI Chatbot FAQs

What LLM APIs do you recommend for business chatbots?

+
For general conversational speed and low cost, we recommend Google Gemini 3.5 Flash or OpenAI GPT-4o-mini. For highly complex reasoning and technical support domains, GPT-4o or Claude 3.5 Sonnet are excellent options. We structure prompt templates to select the most cost-effective model depending on query complexity.

Can the chatbot collect user contact info and push it to my CRM?

+
Yes. We program custom function calling triggers. When a user expresses interest or requests pricing, the chatbot gathers their name, email, and description, structures it into a clean JSON format, and calls a webhook to push it directly to Hubspot, Zoho, or your custom Laravel dashboard database.

How does human handoff work?

+
If the chatbot fails to answer a query within two turns, or if a user specifically requests a human agent, the chatbot triggers a handoff event. It sends a WhatsApp notification or email alert to your team containing the full conversation history, allowing a human rep to step in immediately.
