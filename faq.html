What is a multi-agent system?

+
A multi-agent system splits complex software development or business process goals among separate AI profiles (e.g., Researcher Agent, Writer Agent, Quality Checker Agent). By debating or validating each other's outputs, they achieve vastly higher accuracy than single-prompt models.

What is RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)?

+
RAG is a pipeline that retrieves matching text blocks from your private files (indexed inside a Vector Database) and passes them to the LLM. This provides the AI with real-time, grounded facts to prevent formatting errors or hallucinations.

Why is RAG better than fine-tuning?

+
Fine-tuning requires massive training computation costs and freezes data in place. RAG allows you to update, add, or delete files in your database instantly, guarantees accurate citation paths to original documents, and operates with zero training cycles.

What Vector Databases do you support?

+

How do AI chatbots work?

+
AI chatbots query semantic text layers, evaluate user search intent, retrieve custom records from a CRM database, call logic tools (like n8n hooks), and output natural conversational replies using LLM APIs.
