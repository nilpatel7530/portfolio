What is RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)?

+
RAG is an AI architecture that retrieves relevant context from your private documents before sending a query to the LLM. This guarantees that the AI generates fact-based answers with clear citations rather than fabricating details.

Why is RAG better than fine-tuning?

+
RAG is superior to fine-tuning because it allows instant data updates without expensive retrain cycles and provides verifiable source links. Fine-tuning models is slow, costly, and doesn't guarantee factual accuracy.
